## 1 Introduction
### 1.1 Scope
Based on preliminary requirements initially shared by TEC for this activity [RD1], this document refines the key performance parameters. These updated requirements are primarily valid for the Hopper Ground Demonstrator "Oneiros", which is part of a de-risking activity of the "Huracan" propulsive system [RD2]. Since the engine design will be close to the flight configuration of the Lunar Mission "Hilal", the current constraints and intentions of the "Huracan" engine are taken into account for future utilization of the GMA.

## System requirements

**REQ-00X - Operational gimbal capability** 
The GMA design provides a deflection capability of +/-10° per axis (pitch and yaw) to serve intended GNC manfouvers during operational flight conditions.

**REQ-00X - Max. gimbal capability** 
For off-nominal cases, emergency authority and growth potential of the design, the max. gimbal deflection shall not exceed +/-12°.

**REQ-00X - Rotation axis**
To provide full pitch and yaw control capability, a two-axis rotation shall be provided. Both axis are located perpendicular (90°) with respect to each other in the same vertical plane. 

**REQ-00X - Angular velocity**
To compromise GNC needs with mechanical constraints of the engine such as the actuator capacity, the max. angular velocity of the engine is 20°/s. 

**REQ-00X - Angular acceleration**
The engine´s acceleration and hence, the capability of the GMA shall be max. 25 rad/s².

**REQ-00X - Friction coefficient**
To reduce the breaking torque of the actuators, the GMA shall provide a friction coefficient lower than 0.1 under ambient sea level conditions, a total nominal thrust of 15 kN and thermal steady state conditions of 160K at the gimbal attachment surface at the Injection Head flange. 

**REQ-00X - Geometrical envelope** 
From the vertical center axis of the engine a clearance of the gimbal shall provide a min. and collision-free motion w.r.t. the Ignition system´s main body and it´s periphery of 10mm radially. The maximum  envelope of th GMA is given by the mass constraint, the loads and (thermo-) mechanical stress requirements mentioned in this document.

**REQ-00X - Mass** 
The maximum mass of the GMA shall be equal or below 5kg with an mass measurement accuracy of +/-0,1%.

**REQ-00X - Environment**
Protection of sensitive areas (e.g. areas of relative motion) by sand particles of an average size of 0.2mm at a temperature of 49.6°C [RD3]

**REQ-00X - Lubrication**
- Sea level (0.994 bar to 1.0276 bar and 12% to 91% humidity) [RD3] and vacuum rated (not hard critera for Oreinos)

**REQ-00x - Nominal thrust** 
The GMA shall transmit a total nominal thrust load of 15 kN vertically

**REQ-00x - (Quasi-)static thrust** 
For single events the GMA shall sustain an escessive load of 22.5 kN, which includes a safety factor of 1.5 applied to the nominal thrust
